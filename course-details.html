Optimize Your linkedin Profile

Linkedin was designed to help users build their professional profiles and network with other professionals in their field. Regardless of how busy you are, it is critical to network with others in your field in order to stay ahead. Your connections will also have their own network to which they will connect. If a friend or a collage refers you, they will see a professional profile representing someone they want to work with.

Profile photo

If you dont have your professional photo please ask for assistance.

Let's look at our profile. After you've logged in, go to the top right corner of the navigation page and click on me. A dropdown menu with the option to view profile should appear. Select the "view profile" option.

Then, to upload your profile photo, click on the large circle. Remember that this is the photo that everyone will see, so make it look professional.

Explore the various options available to you to become acquainted with the profile photo editor.
